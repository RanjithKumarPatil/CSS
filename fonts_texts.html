Different font styles and text types

What is font ?

The font determines the shape of the characters. it is the graphical representation of the text. This may include a different typeface, pointsize, weight, color, or design. Properties that affect a text's font, e.g., which font gets applied, its size. and whether it's bold, italic etc

What is Text ?

Text means the way the layout and presentation is computed. Properties that affect the spacing and other layout feaatures of the text, allowing manipulation of, for example the space between lines and letters, and how the text is aligned within the content box
